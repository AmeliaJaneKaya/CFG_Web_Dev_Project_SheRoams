    BANGKOK

    Bangkok, Thailand's capital, is a large city known for ornate shrines and vibrant street lif. The boat-filled Chao Phraya River feeds its network of canals, flowing past the Rattanakosin royal district, home to opulent Grand Palace and its saced at Phra Kaew Temple.Nearby is Wat Pho Temple with an enormous reclining Buddha and, on the opposite shore, Wat Arun Temple with its steep steps and Khmer style spire.

    CHIANG MAI

    Chiang Mai is a city in mountainous nothern Thailand. Founded in 1296, it was capital of the independent Lanna Kingdom until 1558. Its Old City area still retins vestiges of walls and moats from its history as a cultural and religious center. It is also home to hundreds of elaborate Buddhist temples, including 14th century Wat Phra Sigh and 15th century Wat Chedi Luang, adorned with carved serpents.
